About Us

The African Lyceum is a youth-focused platform dedicated to developing critical thinking, public speaking, and leadership skills across Africa. We believe that empowered minds build empowered nations.

Our Vision

To create a generation of African youth who think critically, speak confidently, and lead with integrity to transform the continent.

Our Objectives

  • Train youth in debate, public speaking, and communication skills
  • Promote African history, culture, and intellectual heritage
  • Build a network of young leaders and changemakers
  • Provide platforms for youth to share ideas and solutions

Our Values

  • Integrity: Honesty and ethical leadership in all we do
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standard in learning and action
  • Unity: Celebrating African diversity and collaboration
  • Knowledge: Lifelong learning and intellectual curiosity

Our Goals

  • Launch chapters in 10 African countries within 3 years
  • Train 5,000 youth in leadership and communication programs
  • Host annual African Youth Leadership Summit
  • Build an online resource hub for African youth development
